# Life Safety & Reality Skills Resources

A curated collection of free, high-value resources to improve life skills, personal safety, survival, critical thinking, and situational awareness. Focus on practical knowledge that matters in the real world.

---

## 1. Free Guides, PDFs & Manuals

- FEMA Emergency Management Guides – https://www.fema.gov/emergency-managers (disaster preparedness & response)  
- American Red Cross First Aid, CPR, & Emergency Preparedness – https://www.redcross.org/get-help/how-to-prepare-for-emergencies.html  
- CDC Emergency Preparedness & Response – https://www.cdc.gov/cpr/index.htm  
- Survival Skills Manual – https://www.survivalskills.com/free-guides (free PDFs covering wilderness & urban survival)  
- National Safety Council Safety Manuals – https://www.nsc.org/home-safety/safety-resources  
- Basic Self-Defense & Personal Safety Guides – https://www.wikihow.com/Category:Self-Defense
